About Us

Online Voting System

The Online Voting System is a modern, secure, and transparent platform designed to make democratic participation accessible to everyone.

Our Mission

Our mission is to strengthen democracy by providing a secure, accessible, and transparent platform for online voting. We believe that every citizen has the right to participate in democratic processes without barriers.

Why Online Voting?

  • Accessible: Vote from anywhere, anytime
  • Secure: Advanced security measures protect your data
  • Transparent: Real-time results and verification
  • Fair: Each vote counts equally
  • Efficient: Quick and hassle-free process

How It Works

1. Register

Create your account with valid Aadhaar details

2. Verify

Admin approves your registration

3. Authenticate

Verify your identity using OTP

4. Vote

Cast your vote for your preferred candidate

Security Features

  • OTP Verification: Two-factor authentication for voter verification
  • Aadhaar Validation: Unique identity verification system
  • One Vote Per Voter: System prevents duplicate voting
  • Secret Ballot: Vote secrecy is maintained
